About

The Ramanna Nikaya is one of the three major Buddhist monastic orders (Nikayas) in Sri Lanka, alongside the Siam Nikaya and Amarapura Nikaya. Founded in the 19th century, it was established to preserve the purity of the monastic discipline (Vinaya) and to promote the study and practice of Theravada Buddhism.

Core Values

  • Strict observance of the Vinaya (monastic discipline)
  • Promotion of meditation and mindfulness
  • Preservation and teaching of the original Dhamma
  • Service to the community through Dhamma activities
